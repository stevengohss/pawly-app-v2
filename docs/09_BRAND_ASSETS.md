# Pawly Brand Assets

**Document Version:** Foundation v1.1  
**Status:** Active brand-asset reference

> **Revision v1.1 — 25 Jun 2026:** Included in the sole `/docs` source-of-truth set.

## Purpose

This document records the approved Pawly logo system and production rules so Stitch, Nano Banana, Codex, and future contributors use consistent assets.

## Approved Brand Direction

Pawly is warm, story-led, and emotionally personal.

The logo system should communicate:

- pets
- care
- connection
- love
- belonging

without looking clinical, childish, or generic.

## App Icon

### Primary App Icon

- Terracotta rounded-square background.
- White paw print.
- White dog + cat + heart mark inside main pad.
- Primary use: iOS/Android app icon, favicons, avatars, small icon usage.

### Secondary Light Icon

- Linen White rounded-square background.
- Terracotta paw/dog/cat/heart mark.
- Use: light in-app surfaces, documents, social templates, light promotional use.

## Wordmarks

### Full Wordmark

- Terracotta Pawly wordmark.
- Heart in `a`.
- Dog/cat detail in `w`.
- Use only at larger sizes where detail remains legible.

### Simplified Wordmark

- Terracotta Pawly wordmark.
- Heart in `a`.
- Simplified `w` without pet-detail complexity.
- Use for compact headers, small partner placements, and reduced-size brand layouts.

## Logo Lockups

### Primary Horizontal Logo

```text
[Primary app icon]  [Full wordmark]
```

Use for wide applications: website, decks, splash/onboarding, partner materials, email/signage.

### Stacked Logo

```text
[Primary app icon]
      Pawly
```

Use for square/portrait spaces: social, posters, event cards, onboarding, stickers.

Use simplified wordmark in small stacked lockups.

## Production Rules

- Keep masters as editable `.ai` and `.svg`.
- Export transparent `.png` variants.
- Use flat production fills; do not use generated texture, glow, accidental white fringe, or presentation shadows.
- Primary terracotta: `#D97757`.
- Linen White: `#FAF9F6`.
- White: `#FFFFFF`.
- Do not redraw logo geometry in code.
- Preserve clear space around every lockup.
- Test app icon at 1024px, 256px, 64px, and 32px.

## Validation

Before release/use:

- Check on Linen White and dark blue backgrounds.
- Check transparent-edge quality.
- Check wordmark at small header scale.
- Check app icon on actual iPhone home screen.
- Confirm no white matte/fringe remains.
